Batch Genealogy Tracking

Enables end-to-end batch traceability across raw materials, intermediate processes, and finished cable drums — ensuring structured manufacturing visibility and compliance.

Why It Matters

Cable manufacturing involves multiple raw materials such as copper rods, polymer compounds, steel wires, and additives.

Without batch genealogy tracking, tracing quality issues or compliance gaps becomes complex and time-consuming.

Operational Coverage

Raw Material Linking

Connects raw material batches to production lots.

Process-Level Tracking

Tracks material flow across manufacturing stages.

Drum-Level Mapping

Links finished cable drums to originating material batches.

Recall & Compliance Support

Enables fast issue isolation and regulatory traceability.

Business Impact

Faster Root Cause Analysis

Improved Audit Readiness

Reduced Quality Risk

Stronger Manufacturing Governance

ERP-First Positioning

Batch Genealogy Tracking integrates within ERP manufacturing and quality workflows — strengthening traceability without replacing core ERP systems.
