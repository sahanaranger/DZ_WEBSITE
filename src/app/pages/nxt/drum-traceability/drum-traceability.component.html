Drum-Level Traceability

Ensures traceability at the individual drum level across production, validation, dispatch, and customer delivery — strengthening operational control and manufacturing visibility.

Why It Matters

Cable manufacturing involves multiple stages, validations, and dispatch movements. Without structured traceability, operational transparency is reduced.

Drum-Level Traceability enables structured tracking of each drum throughout the production lifecycle.

Operational Coverage

Production Stage Identification

Structured drum identification during manufacturing stages.

Validation Linkage

Association with stage-wise production validation checkpoints.

Batch Genealogy Alignment

Linkage to batch genealogy records within the manufacturing process.

Dispatch Visibility

Visibility of drum movement across dispatch and delivery lifecycle.

Business Impact

Improved Operational Transparency

Reduced Manual Reconciliation

Stronger Manufacturing Control

Structured Process Governance
