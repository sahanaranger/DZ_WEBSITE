Production Planning & Control

Structured production scheduling, capacity planning, and material coordination for controlled manufacturing execution.

Order-Driven Production Scheduling

Production is triggered based on confirmed sales orders and delivery commitments.

Enables order-wise allocation, priority sequencing, and planned completion timelines aligned with customer commitments.

Capacity & Load Planning

Optimizes machine and manpower capacity across production lines and shifts.

Identifies bottlenecks, balances load, and improves overall factory throughput.

Material Availability Coordination

Validates production plans against raw material and intermediate stock availability.

Prevents shortages and ensures uninterrupted manufacturing execution.

Stage-Wise Production Control

Tracks work-in-progress across each manufacturing stage.

Provides planned vs actual visibility and improves operational accountability.
